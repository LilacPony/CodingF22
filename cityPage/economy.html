Economy

Lethbridge’s diverse economy offers a variety of diverse career opportunities, starting points, and services unique to the city. With a strong industrial presence on the north side of the city, Lethbridge serves as a major agricultural and food-processing hub, with several factories and plants such as McCain’s, alongside easily purchasable farming equipment offered through businesses like UFA. The University of Lethbridge and Lethbridge College act as the city’s educational draw, offering a variety of programs and degrees for a variety of careers and trades. The university even has a campus in Calgary to draw more students to Lethbridge’s educational offerings.

Alongside the several major storefronts that provide access to everyday necessities, Lethbridge has a thriving community of passionate entrepreneurs and independent businesses scattered across the city, though predominantly seen downtown. The Downtown Lethbridge Business Revitalization Zone (BRZ) collaborates with and promotes these businesses in hope of bringing commerce downtown, where you can find everything from creative studios to contract work, cafes, retail, bars, restaurants, and various tourist destinations that guarantee an incredibly sweet experience.
