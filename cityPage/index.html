Welcome to Lethbridge!

Nestled in the calming coulees of southern Alberta and surrounded by vast plains and foothills, this small city offers a plethora of natural delights and historical sites to discover and explore. With plentiful trees spanning each stretch of road and various parks scattered across the city, each adventure through Lethbridge’s streets offers a familiar yet unique experience. Split by the Old Man River, the west, north, and south sides of Lethbridge, alongside its downtown offers a variety of services and recreational spaces to stock up on necessities and enjoy the city’s atmosphere. Brimming with a fascinating history rooted in the river bottom hills, Lethbridge offers a variety of ways to get in touch with the city’s roots in mining. You will learn how that history has shaped the unique structure of the downtown core, and the wide offerings of the city’s arts and culture scene. Alongside that, the welcoming community of long-lived permanent residents and newcomers attending post-secondary at both the University of Lethbridge and Lethbridge College are sure to make your visit an experience to fondly remember.
